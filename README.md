# TempNumber Login Benchmark: evaluating reliability across regions

## 1. Introduction
SMS verification performance varies significantly across regions, operators, and infrastructure quality.

## 2. Regional Coverage
- broad international availability  
- multiple operator routes  
- inventory varies by country  

## 3. OTP Delivery Reliability
- strong performance in major regions  
- moderate variability in developing markets  
- influenced by carrier quality  

## 4. Activation Success Rates
- high success in supported regions  
- inconsistent results in some countries  
- dependent on route selection  

## 5. Regional Stability
- stable in Europe and North America  
- mixed performance in Asia and LATAM  
- sensitive to local infrastructure  

## 6. Conclusion
TempNumber offers broad coverage with acceptable reliability, though performance varies by region.
